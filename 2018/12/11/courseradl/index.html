1.Neural Networks and Deep Learning

week 1

Relu(rectified linear unit)

$max(0,w^Tx+b)$

Different types of neural network:

CNN(Convolutional Neural Network) used often for image application and

RNN(Recurrent Neural Network) used often for one-dimension sequence data such as text transcript.

Hybrid neural network architecture for autonomous driving.

Structured and Unstructured data

  • Structured data refers to things that has a defined meaning, such as price and age. It is easy to be represented by two dimension tables.
  • Unstructured data refers to things like pixel, raw audio, text, which is difficult for computers to understand.
